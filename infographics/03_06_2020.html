What did you watch in lockdown?

  1. Back to Infographics
  2. Published on 03 June, 2020

What do we infer?

Continuing with the daily visualization streak, today we take upon shows which I think were popular in India during the lockdown. By taking a vague assumption, that the viewers might have searched those shows in Google, we take a look at the trends in Google Keyword searches/week for five shows namely- Ramayana, Mahabharata, Money heist, Asur, Paatal Lok. It's no secret that the Indian government's decision to rerun Ramanand Sagar's classic Ramayana and B.R.Chopra's Mahabharata was a masterstroke. According to Business Standard, 'Ramayan' which was re-airing on Doordarshan became the world's most-watched show on April 16 with 77 million viewers. Money heist also created a lot of buzz.
