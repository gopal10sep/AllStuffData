Payment transactions: UPI!

  1. Back to Infographics
  2. Published on 29 June, 2020

What do we infer?

Reserve Bank of India just started publishing daily data for various transaction systems. We just have the data for the month of June. When we take up transactions done using UPI, we can observe that the time series dips down in the weekends. We can use this to make this conclusion that UPI has increasingly been used for businesses across India which impacts its volume and usage on non-business days. UPI has grown leaps and bounds over the last 2-3 years and is expected to grab a larger share in the future as well.
