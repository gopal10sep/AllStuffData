Draconian conflict: Story of India and China's trade balance

  1. Back to Infographics
  2. Published on 09 June, 2020

What do we infer?

Gauging feasibility of some of the so-called intellectual's 'Say-No-To-China' campaign, presenting a visual story sequentially plotting India and China's numbers on GDP, GDP (PPP), GDP annual growth rate; we can easily see how much we are lagging behind the Dragon as of now. Studying numbers of India's ministry of commerce we also see we have been importing more than we have been exporting to China, so the trade deficit continues to grow. In the last few pie-diagrams, I have tried to illustrate the percentage of India's import/export in China's market and vice versa. This clearly shows China won't be losing much even if we boycott China's products, but India will be losing China as a major export market.
